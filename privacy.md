# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

I'm Billy Hart, and this is my personal website. I thought I should be clear about what I do and don't do with information here.

## What I don't do:

- Share your information with anyone
- Sell your information to anyone
- Track you across the web
- Store information I don't need

## What information I collect:

- Basic web server logs (IP addresses, pages visited) for site maintenance
- Any comments or feedback you provide directly to me

## What I might do with your feedback:

- Quote or reference comments you leave on blog posts or LinkedIn
- Use your insights to improve content or share lessons learned
- Give you credit unless you ask me not to
